I am a Second Year Ph.D. student in Finance at Temple University's Fox School of Business, supervised by [Prof. Samuel Rosen](https://sites.google.com/view/samuel-rosen/), [Prof. Gurdip Bakshi](https://sites.google.com/view/gurdipbakshi1/home) and [Prof. Xiaohui Gao Bakshi](https://sites.google.com/site/xiaohuigaobakshi/). I hold an M.Sc. in Finance from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (2023-2025) and a B.Sc. in Mathematics and Statistics from Hong Kong Baptist University (2019-2023). My current research studies how equity markets priced U.S. stablecoin legislation.

**Research Interests:** Empirical Banking; Financial Regulation; FinTech and Digital Assets
