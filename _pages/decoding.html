MY CHINESE NAME

Surname: 闫 (Yán)

  • Pronunciation: IPA: [jɛn]; "yen" (like currency), high-flat tone
  • Original Meaning: "Gatekeeper; dweller near city gates"
  • Character Breakdown: 门 (gate) + 三 (historical variant representing multiple gates)
  • Classical Reference: 《周礼》："閻胥掌比中閻之众"
    ("The Yan official governed those within the inner gates" — Rites of Zhou)
  • Modern Usage: Exclusively a surname, denoting gatekeeper heritage

First Name: 璟 (Jǐng)

  • Pronunciation: IPA: [tɕiŋ]; "jeeng" (soft j + "ing"), falling-rising tone
  • Original Meaning: "Luminous radiance of flawless jade"
  • Character Breakdown: 玉 (jade) + 景 (light/scenery)
  • Classical Reference: 《艺文类聚》："琼瑰璟瑜，灼烁明珠"
    ("Like glowing jade and radiant pearls, their brilliance surpasses stars" — Tang Dynasty anthology)
  • Modern Usage: Poetic term for moral clarity and elegance
